Things placed in [square brackets] below and elsewhere are variables that must/can be placed after the command eg: "@prefix@gen fNPC 2".

@prefix@gen / @prefix@g  [item] -- Procedurally generates an item 
@prefix@itemlist / @prefix@list -- lists the items that can be generated
@prefix@info -- gives some information on the bot
@prefix@addfantasyrace [race] [gender=both] [weight=1] -- adds a fantasy race to your personal list
@prefix@reviewfantasyraces -- lists your fantasy races
@prefix@removefantasyraces [race] or [all] -- deletes a (or all) fantasy race(s) from personal list
@prefix@gbchangeprefix [prefix] -- Changes the bot command prefix for the server, \"!\" by default
@prefix@feedback [feedback] -- Allows you to send me feedback anonymously.
